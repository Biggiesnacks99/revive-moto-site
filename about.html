About Us

Revive Moto Parts is a locally operated motorcycle and powersports shop based in Spencerville, Indiana. We are a Registered Indiana Retail Merchant, authorised to sell and collect sales tax in the state of Indiana.

Our focus is on dependable service, quality parts, and building long‑term relationships with riders and owners in our area. We support a wide range of machines, including Harley‑Davidson, metric motorcycles, ATVs, side‑by‑sides, dirt bikes and other powersports equipment.
